Opportunity for Spotify
See strategic recommendation below ↓
PM recommendation
Build "Spatial Sound" — Dolby Atmos-class immersive audio, natively on Spotify
The single biggest structural reason a premium listener chooses Apple Music over Spotify is Dolby Atmos Spatial Audio — a 3D immersive sound experience that makes familiar tracks feel physically transformed. Spotify rolled out lossless in late 2025, but spatial audio remains entirely absent. This isn't a niche audiophile feature anymore: Apple has trained millions of AirPods users to expect it.

What to build: License or build Spotify Spatial, a Dolby Atmos playback layer for Premium subscribers. Partner with Dolby (or compete via Sony 360 Reality Audio) and invest in a catalogue re-mixing program with major labels — similar to what Apple has already funded.

Why this wins: It closes the last meaningful audio quality gap, removes the strongest "but Apple Music sounds better" objection, and gives Spotify a reason to raise Premium prices again (e.g. a "Spotify Premium Max" tier at $15.99). It also strengthens the value proposition for the AirPods Pro user who currently keeps Apple Music as a second subscription just for Atmos. Turn that person into a Spotify-only user.
