# Spotify: Next Big Product Bet

**To:** Spotify Product Leadership
**Re:** Strategic recommendation — closing the Apple Music quality gap
**Date:** June 2026

---

Spotify's moat is discovery. No competitor comes close on algorithm, social features, or cross-platform reach. But among paying subscribers, one objection keeps resurfacing: *Apple Music sounds better.* That's no longer just audiophile mythology — it's a concrete, demonstrable gap in Dolby Atmos Spatial Audio that Apple has spent years conditioning users to expect.

Spotify should make immersive audio its next major product bet.

The play: launch **Spotify Premium Max** — a higher tier built around Spatial Audio playback, powered by Dolby Atmos licensing and a label co-investment program to accelerate catalogue re-mixing. Price it at $15.99/month. This move does three things simultaneously: it closes the last meaningful quality argument Apple Music owns, it creates a new monetization tier at a moment when margin expansion is the investor story, and it gives the 290 million paid subscribers a tangible reason to upgrade rather than churn.

Spatial Audio is not a niche feature. Apple has shipped it to hundreds of millions of AirPods users. Spotify should stop ceding that ground and go take it.
